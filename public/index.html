Project Description

OrbitCart simulates a wholesaler for aerospace and high-performance lab components. The site combines a storefront catalog, product detail pages, mock checkout, user profiles, and CAD-on-demand “services” to demonstrate HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node/Express.

FAQ

Is OrbitCart a real store?

No. It is a student project for CSC 317, not a real business.

Can I actually order aerospace parts here?

No orders are processed. All products and payments are mock data so we can focus on front-end and back-end skills.

What technologies does OrbitCart use?

HTML, CSS, vanilla JavaScript, Node.js, and Express.js, with a Northrop Grumman–style video hero on the home page.
